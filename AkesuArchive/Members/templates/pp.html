Akesu Archive - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Akesu Archive ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you ("you," "students," or "teachers") when you use our web application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you directly provide to us when you register for an account, create or upload notes, post content, communicate with other users, or otherwise use the App. This information may include:

  • **Account Information:** Your name, email address, username, and password. For teacher accounts, this might also include your school affiliation or role. For student accounts, this might include your class or year group (optional).
  • **Content:** Notes, documents, text, images, and other materials you upload or post on the App.
  • **Communications:** Records of your communications with us, such as support requests or feedback.
  • **Profile Information (Optional):** You may choose to provide additional information in your profile, such as a profile picture or a brief biography.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • **Log Data:** Information about your use of the App, such as the pages you visit, the time and date of your access, your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • **Device Information:** Information about the device you use to access the App, including device model and unique device identifiers.
  • **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use and the content you view or download.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
  • Personalize your experience on the App.
  • Allow teachers to post notes and students to access them.
  • Facilitate communication between users (if applicable features are implemented).
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support.
